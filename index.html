ModerNet

Solutions for building modern websites quickly

This project was recently founded and is growing. Learn more and contribute to the lofty goals of helping people build modern websites quickly!

Authors and Contributors

Eric Swanson (@ericis) founded ModerNet because of the rapidly growing technology solutions available. The rich capabilities of cloud offerings coupled with increasingly powerful browser features provides an opportunity to shake off old patterns of the past and adopt newer, more efficient methods of creating websites. It's an exciting time and we hope you contribute some of your excitement to the project!

Support or Contact

Check out the documentation at https://github.com/modernet or contact @ericis and we'll help you sort it out.
